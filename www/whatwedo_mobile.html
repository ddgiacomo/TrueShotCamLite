What exactly is True Shot CAM? When you take photos and videos using your smartphone, True Shot CAM assigns an identifying number and also stamps the photo or video with the time, date, and GPS coordinates (plus you may type in your own comments). Once a photograph is taken, it is automatically registered in the system. You can use True Shot CAM to compare any copy against the original image, by using its unique identification number.


True Shot CAM is convenient. The app is downloaded to your smartphone, so you can use it where and when you want. You have the option of saving your photos and videos on your True Shot CAM personal album. The personal album, stored on the Cloud to give you easy and convenient access, and is kept completely private from others.

To confirm or verify that an image is authentic, True Shot CAM performs a careful, pixel-by-pixel comparison between the image that has been registered on the True Shot CAM system and the comparison image that you upload.

Users can choose to make their uploaded photos private or public. Both types of photos can be verified. Yet, only public photos can be searched and viewed by other users.

Images can lie. Don’t let them. Use True Shot CAM to register, certify, track, and validate images.
